How it works

Three steps to a coin with a real, rising floor.

Nothing here touches your creator fees, and nothing can ever remove value from the vault except a holder redeeming their own share.

01

Launch your coin

Launch your memecoin however you like — you're the creator and you keep every creator fee. Open a Ballast vault for it and pick which tokenized stocks may back it.

you keep 100% of fees
02

Fund the floor

Buy tokenized stock (AAPLx, NVDAx, TSLAx…) and deposit it into the vault. Every buyback raises the floor and is an on-chain event — provable, not a promise.

reserves only go up
03

Holders redeem

Any holder can burn their coins for a pro-rata slice of the vault's real stock, on-chain, anytime. The floor is enforced by code, and it's preserved for everyone who stays.

the redeemable floor

Safe by construction

The only way value leaves the vault is you redeeming your own share.

Not a policy — a property of the code. There is no withdraw, no admin drain, no sweep. Verified by 25 adversarial tests and two rounds of independent review.

⚓

No drain path exists

The vault is a program-derived account with no private key. Admin and engine keys can configure and add backing — they can never extract a single unit, even if fully compromised.

◈

Real spot, no leverage

Backing is tokenized stock the vault actually holds. Nothing to liquidate, nothing to evaporate on a wick. The floor can't be margin-called.

↻

Always redeemable

Redemption can never be paused — not even by a compromised admin. Your claim on the floor is available on-chain, any time, forever.

◎

Open & verifiable

Open-source, with on-chain proof of reserves anyone can recompute. Independent audit and legal review precede any mainnet launch.
