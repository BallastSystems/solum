How it works

Three steps. You barely do anything.

No staking, no lockups, no forms. Just buy and hold like any memecoin.

01 / BUY

Buy the coin

Ape into a Solum coin like any memecoin on Solana. That's it — no accounts, no sign-up.

02 / STACK

The floor stacks itself

Every trade after that — buys and sells — routes a small tax into buying real tokenized stock for the vault. Your redeemable floor rises with the volume, on-chain and visible.

03 / CASH OUT

Cash out anytime

Ride the pump, or burn your coins for your exact pro-rata slice of the vault's real stocks — whenever you want. The floor is always there.

Why it's safe to hold

The opportunity is real because the vault is untouchable.

◆ Nobody can drain it

The vault is a program-derived account with no private key. Not the team, not a hacked admin — there's no withdraw button in the code. Only you, redeeming your own share.

▲ Real stocks, no leverage

The floor is actual tokenized stock the vault holds. Nothing borrowed, nothing to liquidate on a wick. It can't be margin-called to zero.

↻ Cashing out can't be switched off

Redemption can never be paused — not even by a compromised admin. Your claim on the floor is on-chain, anytime, forever.
