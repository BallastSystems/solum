How it works

Buy. Stake. Get paid in real stock.

A normal pump.fun coin — with its trading fees wired into a real-stock vault you earn from.

01 / BUY

Buy the coin

Ape into a Solum coin like any memecoin on Solana. No accounts, no sign-up.

02 / STAKE

Stake to start earning

Lock your coins in Solum. Every trade's creator fees buy real tokenized stock into an undrainable vault — and your stake accrues a growing claim on it.

03 / CLAIM

Claim real shares

Cash out your accrued Apple, NVIDIA, and Tesla anytime, straight from the vault, on-chain. The longer you held, the bigger the payout.

Why it's safe to hold

Rug-proof by design.

◆ The vault can't be drained

A program-derived account with no private key. Not the team, not a hacked admin — there's no withdraw button in the code. Only you, claiming your own share.

▲ Real stock, no leverage

The vault holds actual tokenized stock — nothing borrowed, nothing to liquidate on a wick. It can't be margin-called to zero.

↻ Claiming can't be switched off

Redemption can never be paused — not even by a compromised admin. Your claim is on-chain, anytime, forever.
