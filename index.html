How it works

Three steps to a real, rising floor.

Nothing touches your creator fees.

01 / LAUNCH

Launch your coin

Launch your memecoin however you like — you keep every creator fee. Open a Ballast vault and pick which tokenized stocks may back it.

you keep 100% of fees
02 / FUND

Fund the floor

Buy tokenized stock and deposit it into the vault. Every buyback raises the floor and is a public, on-chain event.

reserves only go up
03 / REDEEM

Holders redeem

Any holder can burn their coins for a pro-rata slice of the vault's real stock, on-chain, anytime. The floor is enforced by code.

the redeemable floor

Safe by construction

Not a policy — a property of the code.

No withdraw. No admin drain. No sweep.

⚓

NO DRAIN PATH

The vault is a program-derived account with no private key. Admin and engine keys can configure and add backing — they can never extract a single unit, even if fully compromised.

◆

REAL SPOT, NO LEVERAGE

Backing is tokenized stock the vault actually holds. Nothing to liquidate, nothing to evaporate on a wick. The floor can't be margin-called.

↻

ALWAYS REDEEMABLE

Redemption can never be paused — not even by a compromised admin. Your claim on the floor is on-chain, any time, forever.

◎

OPEN & VERIFIABLE

Open-source, with on-chain proof of reserves anyone can recompute. Independent audit and legal review precede any mainnet launch.

Hardened by a 25-check adversarial test suite + a 360-operation invariant fuzzer + two internal security reviews. Devnet · pre-audit.
