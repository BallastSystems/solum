How it works

A normal pump.fun coin — with its trading fees wired into a real-stock vault you win from.

1

Buy the coin

Ape in like any Solana memecoin. No accounts, no sign-up.

2

Hold to earn tickets

Every trade sends real stock into the vault. Hold the coin and you collect tickets — the longer you hold, the more you get.

3

Win the pot — or cash out the floor

Win the growing jackpot of real stock, or redeem your share of the vault anytime. Either way, you keep your coins.

One vault.
Two ways you win.

The stock that piles up never leaves. It does two jobs at once.

The floor · your downside

It can't go to zero

Real stock backs every coin. Redeem your pro-rata share anytime, on-chain — no one can drain it, not even the team. A hard floor under the price.

The jackpot · your upside

Hold, and you can win the pot

A share of every trade fills a prize pot of real stock. Hold the coin to earn tickets; a winner is drawn and claims the pot. No-loss — your coins stay yours.

Watch the vault fill

Fees become stock and lock in. The floor rises, the pot grows — and nothing takes value out except a holder claiming their share.
