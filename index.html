How it works

Three steps to a coin with a real, rising floor. Nothing here touches your creator fees.

01

Launch your coin

Launch your memecoin however you like — you keep every creator fee. Open a Ballast vault and pick which tokenized stocks may back it.

you keep 100% of fees
02

Fund the floor

Buy tokenized stock — AAPLx, NVDAx, TSLAx — and deposit it into the vault. Every buyback raises the floor and is a public on-chain event.

reserves only go up
03

Holders redeem

Any holder can burn their coins for a pro-rata slice of the vault's real stock, on-chain, anytime. The floor is enforced by code.

the redeemable floor

Safe by construction

The only way value leaves the vault is you redeeming your own share. Not a policy — a property of the code.

⚓

No drain path

The vault is a program-derived account with no private key. Admin keys can configure and add backing — they can never extract a single unit, even if fully compromised.

◈

Real spot, no leverage

Backing is tokenized stock the vault actually holds. Nothing to liquidate, nothing to evaporate on a wick. The floor can't be margin-called.

↻

Always redeemable

Redemption can never be paused — not even by a compromised admin. Your claim on the floor is on-chain, any time, forever.

Hardened by a 25-check adversarial test suite + two internal security reviews. Independent audit precedes any mainnet launch.
