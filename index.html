How it works

Three steps. That's the whole thing.

Not a launchpad — you bring a coin you already have.

01 / ADD

Add a vault to your coin

You've got a memecoin. Attach a Solum vault to it in a few clicks. We never touch your coin or the fees you earn.

you keep 100% of fees
02 / FILL

Fill it with real stocks

Buy real stocks like Apple or NVIDIA on-chain and drop them in the vault. The more you add, the higher your coin's floor — and it can never fall.

reserves only go up
03 / CASH OUT

Holders cash out anytime

Any holder can trade their coins back for a real share of those stocks, straight from the vault, whenever they want. That's the floor.

a price it can't crash below

Why it's safe

Rug-proof by design.

No withdraw button. No admin drain. No way to switch off cashing out.

◆

THE VAULT CAN'T BE DRAINED

Not even by you. Once stock is in, the only way it leaves is a holder cashing out their own share. There is simply no "withdraw" button in the code.

▲

REAL STOCKS, NO GAMBLING

The vault holds actual stock — nothing borrowed, nothing leveraged. There's nothing that can get liquidated on a bad candle.

↻

CASHING OUT CAN'T BE BLOCKED

Redemption can never be paused — not even by a hacked admin. A holder's claim is on-chain, anytime, forever.

◎

ANYONE CAN CHECK IT

Fully open-source, with live proof of exactly what's in the vault that anyone can verify. Independent audit and legal review come before any mainnet launch.
