Who wins is drawn on-chain from the committed holder snapshot and Switchboard VRF — anyone can verify the draw. The winner connects their wallet and claims to confirm it's them; their tokenized-stock prize is then delivered within 24 hours. Odds are the winner's share of the hour's ticket pool — their time-averaged $SOLUM balance over the whole hour, so a last-second buy can't tilt a draw.
